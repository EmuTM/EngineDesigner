Engine control

The Engine control is the most essential tool and controls all other tools. It is used to "turn" the engine; the user can set the engine's crankshaft angle or the RPM. Any change in Engine control will affect charts and computations in other tools.

 

Engine control: setting the crankshaft angle

 

When setting the RPM, the "Current accuracy" label shows how many degrees the crankshaft will turn for every screen refresh. The refreshing interval is fixed, so the crankshaft must be turn at a different angle to meet the RPM setting. This does not affect any computation where the RPM setting is taken as input.
